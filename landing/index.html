Addresses for Everyone

A community-driven addressing system for areas without official street addresses. Helping delivery riders, emergency services, and residents navigate neighborhoods worldwide.

Open the Map View on GitHub

Why Community Addresses?

Fill the Gaps

Billions of people live in areas without official addresses. We create temporary, community-verified addresses until official systems catch up.

Community Powered

Local residents know their neighborhoods best. Vote on address claims, add access notes, and help improve navigation for everyone.

Delivery Ready

Designed for delivery riders and logistics companies. Clear addresses with access notes help packages reach their destination.

Open Source

Built on OpenStreetMap data and fully open source. Transparent algorithms and community governance ensure fairness.

Global Reach

Starting in Uganda and expanding worldwide. Our system adapts to local naming conventions and addressing patterns.

Privacy First

No tracking, no personal data collection. Phone numbers are hashed, and we only store what's needed for addresses.

How It Works

1

Find a Building

Navigate to any building on the map. Buildings without official addresses are highlighted.

2

Get an Address

Our algorithm generates a community address based on nearby streets and landmarks.

3

Verify & Improve

Community members can vote on addresses, add access notes, and mark building entrances.

4

Use & Share

Share the address with delivery services, friends, or emergency responders.
